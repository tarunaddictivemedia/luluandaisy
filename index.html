At Lulu & Daisy we believe in creating real food

that’s made in a real kitchen and not in a lab or a factory. When dogs eat real, human-grade food with natural ingredients and no junk, they eat better and their health visibly improves. 

Packaged dog food that’s made in a food factory is in fact, hardly ‘food’.

Read the back of the label and you’ll find it’s filled with nasties like artificial colors, preservatives, corn syrup and synthetics and other unpronounceables that no dog or (even human!) diet needs.
